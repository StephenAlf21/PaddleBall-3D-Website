System Requirements

Check if your rig is ready for the arena.

Minimum Specs

For a stable experience at 1080p with low settings.

  • Processor: Intel i5 6700 or AMD Ryzen 5 7640HS
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1050 Ti or AMD RX 570
  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended Specs

For a smooth experience at 1080p/1440p with high settings.

  • Processor: Intel i7 11700k or AMD Ryzen 9600X
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 3080 or AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT
  • OS: Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • Storage: 2 GB available space (SSD Recommended)

Additional Notes

Game Options: The game includes a comprehensive options menu to adjust graphics quality, resolution, audio levels, and camera settings to best suit your system.

Controller Support: Full controller support is included. We recommend using a controller for the best gameplay experience, especially for local multiplayer.

macOS & Linux: While official support is for Windows, the game may run on other platforms via compatibility layers like Proton, but performance is not guaranteed.
