About Paddle Ball 3D

Paddle Ball 3D is brought to you by Alfred Games, a passionate indie game studio focused on creating immersive and challenging experiences. Inspired by the classic Pong game, Paddle Ball 3D takes the simple, beloved mechanics of paddleball and expands them into a fully 3D, vibrant environment filled with exciting new features and challenges.

Whether you're playing against AI or challenging your friends, Paddle Ball 3D offers something for everyone. Our goal is to create fun and memorable gaming moments for players of all skill levels.
