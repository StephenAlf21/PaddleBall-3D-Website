About The Project

The minds (and machines) behind Paddle Ball 3D

Our Story

Alfred Games is a passionate indie game studio focused on creating immersive and challenging experiences. Inspired by the classic Pong game, Paddle Ball 3D takes the simple, beloved mechanics of paddleball and expands them into a fully 3D, vibrant environment filled with exciting new features and challenges. We believe in the power of simple concepts evolved with modern technology to create something truly special.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create fun and memorable gaming moments for players of all skill levels. We are committed to building games that are easy to learn but hard to master, providing endless hours of competitive fun and replayability. This project leverages both human creativity and cutting-edge AI to deliver a unique and polished experience.
