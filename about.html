About The Project

The story behind the creation of Paddle Ball 3D.

Our Story

Paddle Ball 3D started as a passion project to recreate and modernize the classic arcade experience of Pong. We wanted to build a game that was not only a nostalgic throwback but also a challenging and feature-rich experience for today's gamers. The result is a dynamic, 3D paddle game with multiple game modes, an adaptive AI, and immersive audio that brings the classic gameplay to a new dimension.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver a polished and engaging gameplay experience that is both easy to pick up and difficult to master. We are focused on creating a game with high replayability, offering various modes like Time Attack and Endless Mode to keep players challenged. Through a combination of classic mechanics and modern features, we aim to provide endless hours of fun for players of all skill levels.
